According to the laws of China, if a foreigner stays in a place other than a hotel, he/she should handle registration with the public security organ where the place is by himself/herself or the accommodator within 24 hours after check-in

If a foreigner declares lodging registration by himself/herself, he/she should click “I’m a foreigner” to register. For an accommodator, he/she should click “I’m a house owner” to key in the foreigner’s information
